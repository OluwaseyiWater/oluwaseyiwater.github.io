Oluwaseyi E. Giwa

Incoming PhD Student at UofT

Hi 👋! I broadly work on areas in wireless communications. More specifically on AI-Native Wireless Networks, i.e., how to make AI a core part in—both the architecture and functionality—of wireless networks. I have applied reinforcement learning (RL) and large language models (LLMs) as tools to simultaneously enhance the performance and address the challenges of wireless networks, particularly the forthcoming 6G network. Presently, I am exploring causality in wireless networks in collaboration with Christo K. Thomas.

I am an incoming PhD student at the Edward S. Rogers Sr. Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, University of Toronto advised by Raviraj Adve.

Before that, I obtained my MSc at the University of Cape Town, where I was blessed with amazing mentors: Professor Jonathan Shock and Dr. Jaco Du Toit. In addition to this, I was also fortunate to have worked with Dr. Muhammad Ali Jamshed from the University of Glasgow. Prior to this, I graduated as the best student from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Olabisi Onabanjo University with a departmental CGPA record for my undergraduate degree.


Feel free to reach out to talk about anything through my email at oluwaseyi@aims.ac.za. In case you don’t get a response after two weeks, do not hesitate to send a reminder as your email may have been routed to my junk folder. Also, you can directly schedule a 30-minute chat with me here Calendly.
